HOME Guide for using Drought Guard

Introduction

What we do

We provide drought index insurance that is tailored to fit the needs of your specific situation. By using features including type of crop, duration of coverage, and level of drought, we can ensure that your crops are fully protected at the lowest possible rate.

How we’re different

Drought Index Insurance is a new form of insurance, called parametric insurance, in which payment is dependent on the adverse weather event, rather than the damage that the weather event causes. This makes it easier to insure your crops as you only need to choose a level of drought to protect your crops against, pay for the coverage, and sleep easy knowing that your payment will be automatically issued if the weather triggers are met.

Advantages

With our weather index insurance model, you don’t have to have your farm monitored to make sure you comply with the behaviors to stay valid for insurance or undergo lengthy damage assessments by traditional insurance companies. Since our coverage is based on pre-agreed weather conditions, our insurance plans are more transparent, fair, and faster than traditional insurance.
